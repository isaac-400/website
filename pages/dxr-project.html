Project Zephyrus - VR wind visualization

Inspired by other web visualizations of air currents, Alex Wong, Roger Dai and I created this VR interactive visualization for our VR and AR design class at Dartmouth. The visualization models real airflow data onto 3D terrain which the user can manipulate to better understand weather patterns. The demo runs on the Oculus Quest 1.
